REVIEW PAGE

1. "Building a Business"

Author : Gabriel Rodriquez

This book covers essential topics such as business planning,marketing strategies,financial management and leadership skills.with practical insights and real world examples.

2. "The Power of Habit"

Author : Charles Duhigg

It explores the science behind habits--how they are formed,how they can be changed,and how they impact individual lives and organizational behavior.The book drawa on research from various fields,including psychology,neuroscience,and behavioral economics,to provide insights into why habits exist and how they can be transformed.

3. "Sustainable Business Development"

Author : Charles Wright

This book emphasizes the importance of balancing economic growth with environmental stewardship and social responsibility.Wright provides insights into how business can create value,reduce risks and enhance competitiveness by embracing sustainability as a core business strategy.

4. "Rework"

Author : Jason Fried

The book encourages entrepreneurs and business leaders to rethink traditional business practices,embrace simplicity,and focus on what really matters.Rework challenges conventional wisdom about starting and running a business.

5. Made to Stick

Author : Chip Health and Dan Health

"Made to Stick" explores the characteristics of ideas that are memorable and impactful.The book combines storytelling,research,and practical advice to help individuals and organizations communicate their ideas more effectively.

6. "The ONE Thing"

Author : Gary Keller and Jay Papasan

The book emphasizes the power of focus and concentration,urging readers to simplify their priorities and eliminate distractions to achieve greater productivity and fulfillment

7. "Good to Great

Author: Jim Collins

"Good to Great" examines why some companies excel and sustain superior performance over the long term while others struggle or fail to achieve greatness.

8. "Strengths Finder

Author : Tom Rath

It introduces readers to the concept of focusing on strengths rather than weaknesses to improve personal and professional success.This book helps to discover natural talents and use them to succeed in life.

9. "Just Listen"

Author : Mark Goulston

Just Listen by Mark Goulston is a communication-focused self-help book that aims to improve relationships and connections.The book offers practical techniques and strategies for improving communication skills,building trust,and resolving conflicts in personal and professional relationships.

10. "Financial Intelligence

Author: "Karen Berman and Joe Knight

The book explains the basic financial statements--income statement,balance sheet,and cash flow statement in simple terms.The book teaches how analyze financial data to make better business decisions and avoid common mistakes.

11. "Harry Beckwith

Author: "Selling the Invisible

It is a book that offers practical advice on marketing services rather than physical products.It explores how businesses can effectively market and differentiate intangible services such as consulting, healthcare, financial services, and more.

12. "Career Tracker

Author : Craig Britton

This Book is designed to guide readers on how to plan their careers strategically and make the most of opportunities.It covers topics such as networking,resilience,and maintaining a positive mindset to overcome obstacles and find long-term career fulfillment.
